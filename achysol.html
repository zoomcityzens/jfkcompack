About Achysol Design Concept

Achysol Design Concept is a dynamic Architectural firm that is into building, design and construction consisting of experienced Architects and Builders who can help in designingand construction of tasteful low cost housing units.

Achysol Design Concept LIMITED has successful years of design, business and construction experience, fully conversant with timely delivery, high quality and safety standards for our projects..

We have been involved in diverse projects since the incorporation of the company in 2013. Between this period of establishment and 2018, our lead architect worked with one of the fast rising Real estate company in Lagos to be able to acquire more knowledge and expertise while still running the company by the side. Achysol designed concept stated full operation in 2020 and we have been involved in designing/ supervision/construction of various residential buildings
